Our Aims with Language Conquests-EFL programmes

The mission of Language Conquests – EFL, Cyprus is to provide purposeful and innovative language programmes for international adult students.

Aims:
  • Language Conquests – EFL, Cyprus, aims to develop English language skills in the professional and social contexts tailored to the needs and interests of international adult students. It further aims to build up confidence in the use of General English and English For Specific Purposes.
  • Language Conquests – EFL, Cyprus aims to provide the personal and dependable service from staff of highly qualified and experienced teachers.
  • Language Conquests – EFL, Cyprus aims to ensure that all its English language learners receive the high input of language knowledge essential to their requirements

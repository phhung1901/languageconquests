EFL Programmes

Language Conquests – EFL programmes at ENGLISH STUDY CENTRE in Limassol, Cyprus

We offer the unique opportunity to combine English Language study with travel and the discovery of new places and different cultures. Learn and enrich your English by taking one of the many Language Conquests – EFL courses. Whatever your reason – career development, further study, personal interest or for sheer holiday English enjoyment, Language Conquests – EFL will take you up to the next level.

We are situated in an exclusive residential area just outside the Centre of Limassol – the second largest city in the south of the magnificent Mediterranean island Cyprus.

Take this opportunity! Come as a Language Traveller to a place of sea, sun and fun!

Our school

English Study Centre, founded in 1983 by Krini Askanis, is a highly reputable and well-established English Language learning institute in Cyprus.  The school is renowned for its quality teaching and the wide variety of courses it offers to small groups of adults.

Furthermore, the school is known for its flexibility and creativity in designing and teaching specialised "niche courses". The school prides itself in being one of the most professional on the island offering a high level of customer service.

Our courses

Our language course packages include 3 hours of classes Monday - Friday, taught in small groups by our qualified and experienced teachers; course materials; airport transfers; daily hotel - school transfer; certificate of attendance; and two local tours.

Our customized topics

We offer English for agro tourists (focus on culture, art, history and tradition), English for carers, English for the sports and fitness industry, English for the hospitality industry, Business English, General English and IELTS (International English Language Testing Systems) preparation courses

Cost

The cost of a 15-hour programme is 350 Euros, the cost of a 30-hour programme is 600 Euros. Accommodation varies between 43 and 176 Euros per night depending on the option chosen.

Our management and experience

The Director - Krini Askanis is originally from Britain and has been teaching English since 1970. In Athens, where she taught for a number of years at a well known private high school, and designed EFL courses for in-company programmes, she was awarded the DELTA diploma (Diploma of English Language Teaching for Adults). Her high level of training and her extensive teaching experience have enabled her to enjoy a long-standing reputation in the field.

The Marketing and Sales Manager - Mary Anglberger is originally from Austria but has been teaching English while traveling the world since 1995. She is fluent in English, French and German and has also worked in the fields of tourism/hospitality and international school administration. Currently she is teaching in Latvia but has decided to move to Cyprus to work with the Director of English Study Centre to promote this unique way of language learning.

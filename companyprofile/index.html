Learn about English at Language Conquests

English Study Centre established in 1983 by Krini Askanis, a native British-trained language teacher, is primarily an English as a Foreign Language institute approved by the Ministry of Education in Cyprus and a registered centre for LCCI and EDI Exams qualifications. The school undertakes to teach the English language to adults and children from second grade primary school to third lyceum secondary school. The institute has grown to become a highly reputable institute in Limassol and its field of instruction embraces many varieties of the English language: English as a Foreign Language, English For Specific Purposes, preparation for British exams, as well as academic exam courses at GCE “A” Level in five subjects:
  • English Literature,
  • English Language,
  • Classical Civilisations,
  • Modern Greek
  • and General Studies.
The objectives of English Study Centre are:
  • to offer quality teaching from highly qualified teachers in the four skills of the English Language.
  • for the students to acquire a sound knowledge of grammatical and functional aspects of the English language.
  • for the students to perform with appropriacy, readiness and fluency both in the spoken and written modes.
  • for the students to appreciate the English language in its cultural and social contexts.
English Study Centre organises learning groups large enough to be stimulating but small enough to care about individuals. It is an institute well known for both its friendly, family atmosphere and its high-teaching standards.
The school is located in Ayia Phyla, a quiet residential area just 10 minutes from the sea. The school comes equipped with spacious classrooms, computer laboratory, library, media room, landscaped gardens, swimming pool and a small cafeteria.
Additional facilities include:
  • Free (wifi) Internet and e-mail access.
